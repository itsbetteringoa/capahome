Detailed Pricing Breakdown

1. OpenPhone - BEST OVERALL VALUE ⭐

  • Starter: $15/user/month
    • Unlimited US/Canada calls & SMS
    • Shared phone numbers
    • Voicemail transcriptions
    • 7,000+ integrations via Zapier/Make
  • Business: $23/user/month
    • Auto-attendant (IVR)
    • CRM integrations
    • Analytics
  • Scale: $35/user/month
    • AI call tags
    • Priority support

Why Choose: Best balance of features, price, and usability. No hidden fees.

2. Google Voice - BUDGET OPTION

  • Personal: FREE (US only, limited features)
  • Starter: $10/user/month + $7 Google Workspace = $17/month
    • Max 10 users
    • US calls & texts only
  • Standard: $20/user/month + $7 Workspace = $27/month
  • Premier: $30/user/month + $7 Workspace = $37/month

Why Choose: If you're already in Google ecosystem and need basic functionality. ⚠️ Warning: Texting only works in US, even on paid plans!

3. Grasshopper - GOOD FOR SOLO USE

  • True Solo: $14/month
    • 1 user, 1 number, 1 extension
  • Solo Plus: $25/month
    • Unlimited users, 1 number, 3 extensions
  • Small Business: $55/month
    • Unlimited users, 4 numbers, unlimited extensions
    • Extra numbers: $9/month

Why Choose: Good for solo entrepreneurs with simple needs. ⚠️ Limitation: No CRM integrations, no shared numbers.

4. Zoom Phone - BEST FOR VIDEO TEAMS

  • US & Canada Metered: $10/user/month
    • Metered calling (pay per minute outbound)
    • SMS/MMS included
  • US & Canada Unlimited: $15/user/month
    • Unlimited outbound calls
  • Global Select: $20/user/month
    • Unlimited calling in 40+ countries

Why Choose: Perfect if your team already uses Zoom meetings. ⚠️ Limitation: Base plan charges for outbound calls!

5. Nextiva - FEATURE-RICH BUT COSTLY

  • Core: $15/user/month
    • 100 SMS/MMS per user per month limit
    • Basic IVR
  • Engage: $25/user/month
    • Unlimited call recording
    • 1,000 toll-free minutes
  • Power Suite CX: $75/user/month
    • Call center features

Why Choose: Comprehensive features for larger teams. ⚠️ Limitation: SMS limits and CRM integrations cost extra!

Quick Recommendations

For Startups & Small Businesses

Choose OpenPhone - Best value, unlimited SMS, great integrations, easy setup.

For Personal Use

Choose Google Voice Free - Can't beat free, but limited to US texting.

For Solo Entrepreneurs

Choose Grasshopper - Simple, straightforward, good for one person.

For Zoom-Heavy Teams

Choose Zoom Phone - Seamless integration with existing Zoom workflows.

For Established Businesses with Budget

Choose Nextiva - Most comprehensive, but watch for add-on costs.

Key Considerations

✅ Must-Have Features:

  • Unlimited SMS send/receive
  • Both SMS and MMS support
  • Mobile & desktop apps
  • Quick setup (under 10 minutes)
  • Reliable uptime

⚠️ Watch Out For:

  • Hidden costs (workspace subscriptions, add-ons)
  • SMS message limits
  • US-only texting restrictions
  • Extra fees for toll-free numbers
  • Limited storage for recordings
